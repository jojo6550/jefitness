Section 01
Getting Started

To access your Trainer Portal, log in to JE Fitness with your trainer credentials. You will be automatically redirected here on your first login. After that, you can always return via the ? button in the portal header.

  1. Open a browser and go to the JE Fitness app URL.
  2. Click Log In and enter your trainer email and password.
  3. On first login you'll land here for onboarding. Click Go to Dashboard when ready.
  4. The portal loads your schedule automatically. You're ready to go.
If you see an "Access Denied" message, contact your administrator — your account may need the Trainer role enabled.
Section 02
Dashboard Overview

The Trainer Portal is organized into four tabs, each giving you a focused view of a different part of your work.

Schedule

View upcoming sessions and your appointment archive. Mark sessions as On Time, Late, or No Show.

Clients

Browse every client who has booked with you. View contact details, appointment history, and medical notes.

Availability

Set your weekly recurring schedule. Define the hours and slot capacity for each day of the week.

Notifications

Choose how you're notified about new bookings — instant individual emails or a midnight daily digest.

The refresh button reloads data for the current tab. The ? button reopens this guide at any time. The power button logs you out.

Section 03
Setting Your Availability

Clients can only book appointments during the hours you define here. You must set your availability before any bookings can be made.

  1. Click the Availability tab in the portal header.
  2. A grid shows all 7 days of the week. Toggle each day active or inactive using the toggle switch on the left.
  3. For each active day, set your Start Hour (earliest bookable time) and End Hour (latest bookable time) using the dropdowns. Times are in 24-hour format.
  4. Set the Slot Capacity — the maximum number of clients that can book any single 1-hour slot. The default is 6; the range is 1–50.
  5. When finished, click Save Schedule. You must save before switching tabs or changes will be lost.
Days that are toggled inactive will not be available for booking, even if hours were previously set. Toggle a day on to re-enable it.
Example: Set Monday to 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM with capacity 4 means up to 4 clients can book every hour slot between 8 AM and 4 PM on Mondays.
Section 04
Managing Your Schedule

The Schedule tab is your day-to-day view of all client sessions.

  • Active view — shows all sessions with a scheduled status. These are upcoming sessions waiting for a status update from you.
  • Archive view — read-only history of sessions marked completed, cancelled, or no-show. Use this to review past records.
  • Use the search bar to filter the list by a client's name in real time.

For each session in the Active view, you have three status actions:

On Time

Client attended and the session ran as scheduled. Marks the appointment completed.

Late

Client arrived late but the session took place. Records the session as late with a timestamp.

No Show

Client did not attend. Records the session as no-show with a timestamp.

A confirmation dialog appears every time you change a session's status. This prevents accidental taps. Click Confirm to proceed or Cancel to go back.
Section 05
Viewing Your Clients

The Clients tab lists everyone who has ever booked a session with you, along with their activity status.

  1. Click the Clients tab to load your client roster.
  2. Use the search bar to find a client by name.
  3. Click any client card to open their full detail view.

The client detail modal shows:

  • Contact information — email, phone number, date of birth, gender
  • Appointment stats — total sessions booked vs. completed
  • Medical notes — any notes or uploaded documents on file for the client
Client data is read-only in the portal. To update a client's account or medical records, contact your administrator.
Section 06
Email Notification Preferences

You control how you're notified when clients book appointments. Go to the Notifications tab and choose one of two options:

Option A
Individual email per booking

As soon as a client books, you receive an email immediately with the client's name, date, and time of the appointment.

Option B
Daily digest at midnight

One summary email sent at midnight each night listing all clients scheduled for that day — no interruptions during the day.

  1. Click the Notifications tab.
  2. Select your preferred option using the radio button.
  3. Click Save Preference. A confirmation toast confirms it was saved.
The daily digest is sent to the email address on your trainer account. If you're not receiving emails, check your spam folder or contact admin to verify your email is correct.
Section 07
Tips & Reminders

Keep these rules in mind when managing your schedule and working with clients.

You cannot be booked on days where your availability is set to inactive.
Clients must book at least 1 day in advance. Same-day bookings are not allowed.
Each client can only have one appointment per day, regardless of trainer or time.
All time slots are on the hour only — e.g. 9:00 AM, 10:00 AM, 2:00 PM.
When a slot reaches your configured capacity, no further bookings are accepted for that hour.
To update your account details, password, or email — contact your administrator.
