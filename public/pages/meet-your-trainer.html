Expert guidance tailored to your fitness journey. Meet our team and manage your schedule in one place.

How to Book

  1. Click New Booking at the top-right of the Your Schedule section.
  2. Select a trainer from the dropdown.
  3. Choose a date — must be tomorrow or later.
  4. Pick a time — only the trainer's available hours on that day appear.
  5. Add optional notes, click Confirm Booking, then approve the confirmation pop-up.

Booking Policies

  • Active subscription required — account must be on an active or trialing plan.
  • One appointment per day — you can only have one non-cancelled booking per calendar day.
  • Book at least one day in advance — same-day bookings are not accepted.
  • On-the-hour only — times like 7:00 or 9:00 are valid; half-hours are not.
  • Slot capacity — each hourly slot holds up to 6 clients by default (trainers may adjust this).
  • Trainer hours vary by day — a trainer may be off on certain weekdays or have a limited window (e.g. 7:00–15:00).

Common Errors & How to Fix Them

Error message What it means Fix
Active subscription required Your subscription is inactive or expired. Go to Subscription and upgrade your plan.
You can only book one appointment per day You already have an upcoming booking on that date. Pick a different date, or cancel your existing appointment first.
Appointments must be booked at least one day in advance You selected today or a past date. Choose tomorrow or any future date.
Appointments can only be booked on the hour A non-hour time was submitted (shouldn't happen via the dropdown — may be a browser issue). Reload the page and try again using the time dropdown.
Time slot is fully booked (max 6) That hour with that trainer is at full capacity. Pick a different hour or a different day.
Trainer is only available from X:00 to Y:00 on this day The time you chose is outside the trainer's posted hours for that weekday. Select a time within the trainer's available window shown in the dropdown.
Trainer is not available on this day The trainer has no schedule set for that weekday. Choose a different date or a different trainer.
