8-Week EDS Safe Strength & Fat Loss Program Beginner

A safe and effective program designed for individuals with EDS (Ehlers-Danlos Syndrome).

Program Overview

This program is tailored for individuals with EDS, focusing on joint stability, controlled movements, and gradual progression.

  • Joint-friendly exercises with proper stabilization
  • Progressive strength building with safety as priority
  • Fat loss through controlled movements
  • Avoid hyperextension and dangerous ranges of motion

Frequency: 3-4 days/week. Duration: 45-60 min/session.

Always consult with your healthcare provider before starting any exercise program.
