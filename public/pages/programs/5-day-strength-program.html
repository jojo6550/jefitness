5 Day Strength Program Advanced

Challenge your body to move massive amounts of weight in a controlled and methodical manner that reduces fatigue accumulation in muscles and CNS.

Program Overview

This program is designed for advanced lifters to build strength while minimizing fatigue. Focus on progressive overload and proper form.

  • Build overall strength
  • Reduce CNS fatigue
  • Improve muscle recovery
  • Enhance pressing and pulling power

Recommended Frequency: 5 days per week (with rest days as needed)

Equipment: Barbell, dumbbells, cable machine, leg press, etc.

Day 1 – Lower (Squat focus)

Focus on quads, glutes.

  • 1. Back Squats (Heavy) - 5x5 or 4x4 @ 75-80% of max
  • 2. Lunges - 3 rounds of 12 steps
  • 3. Hack Squat - 4x8
  • 4. Leg Press - 4x8
  • 5. Leg Extensions - 4x8
  • 6. Box stepups - 4x8

Day 2 – Upper (Push focus)

Focus on chest, shoulders, triceps, pressing strength.

  • 1. Barbell Bench Press (Heavy) - 5x5 @ 70-80% of max
  • 2. Incline Dumbbell Press - 4x8
  • 3. Chest Flies - 4x8
  • 4. Overhead Press - 4x8
  • 5. Lateral Raise - 4x8
  • 6. Tricep Pushdowns - 4x8
  • 7. Skull Crushers - 4x8
  • 8. Pushups - AMRAP

Day 3 – Upper (Pull focus)

Focus on back, traps, biceps, grip.

  • 1. Deadlifts (Heavy) - 5x5 @ 70-80% of max
  • 2. Pullups/Lat Pulldowns (Wide) - 3x6
  • 3. Cable Rows (Narrow) - 3x6
  • 4. Shrugs - 4x10
  • 5. Rear delt flies or face pulls - 4x8
  • 6. Bicep Curls - 4x8
  • 7. Hammer Curls - 4x8
  • 8. Wrist Curls - 4x12

Day 4 – Lower (Hinge focus)

Focus on glutes, hamstrings, posterior chain, core.

  • 1. Romanian Deadlifts - 4 x 8
  • 2. Hip thrusts - 4x8
  • 3. Good mornings - 4 x 8
  • 4. Kettlebell Swings - 4 x 10
  • 5. Back extensions - 4 x 10
  • 6. Planks - 4 x 45 secs
  • 7. Farmer's Carries - 4 rounds of 12 steps
  • 8. Leg Raises - 4 x 10

Day 5 – Full Upper / Weak Point Day

Mix of push + pull or focus on lagging areas.

  • 1. Close-grip bench press - 3 x 6
  • 2. Wide and Narrow Lat Pulldowns - 4 x 8
  • 3. Barbell Curl (standing, strict) – 4×6–8
  • 4. Hammer Curl – 3×10–12 (brachialis + forearm carryover to grip strength)
  • 5. Cable Pushdowns (rope or straight bar) – 3×10–12
  • 6. Dips (weighted if strong enough) – 3×AMRAP (strength + lockout power)
  • 7. Zottman Curls – 2×12 (eccentric forearm + biceps control)
  • 8. JM Press (light/moderate) – 2×10 (bench lockout strength, triceps focus)
  • 9. Farmer’s Carries (heavy, 20–30m) – 2–3 rounds (grip + arm stability)
