Upper & Lower Back Program

Strengthen your entire back for better posture, stability, and pain relief.

Upper Back – Thoracic Spine

Focus on improving mobility and strength in the upper back, which is crucial for shoulder health and posture.

1. Cat Cow Stretch

Start on all fours. Inhale and arch your back, dropping your belly and lifting your chest and tailbone (Cow). Exhale and round your spine, tucking your chin and pelvis (Cat).

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10 | Rest: 1 min

2. Thread the Needle

Start in a tabletop position, i.e., on hands and knees. Slide your right arm under your left arm, reaching as far as possible and lowering your right shoulder and ear to the ground. Breathe deeply and hold the stretch. Return to start, then switch sides.

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10 (each side), holding 10–20 seconds per rep | Rest: 1 min

3. Thoracic Extensions

Sit on a chair or stand. Place your hands behind your head. Gently arch your upper back and lift your chest without overextending your lower back.

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10 | Rest: 1 min

4. Wall Angels

Stand with your back against a wall, feet a few inches away. Bend arms to 90° like a goalpost. Slowly raise and lower your arms, keeping elbows, wrists, and back in contact with the wall.

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10 | Rest: 1 min

**Tip:** If you can’t keep contact with the wall, start with Floor Angels lying on your back.

Lower Back – Lumbar Spine

Strengthen your core and lower back muscles to enhance stability and prevent discomfort.

1. Dead Bugs

Lie on your back, arms pointing up, knees bent at 90°. Lower your right arm and left leg simultaneously while keeping your lower back pressed into the floor. Return and repeat on the other side.

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10 per side | Rest: 1 min

2. Bird Dogs

Begin on all fours. Extend your right arm and left leg fully while keeping your back flat. Hold briefly, then return and switch sides.

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10 per side | Rest: 1 min

3. Glute Bridge

Lie on your back, knees bent, feet flat. Press through your heels to lift your hips, forming a straight line from shoulders to knees. Squeeze glutes, hold briefly, then lower.

Sets: 3 | Reps: 10 | Rest: 1 min
